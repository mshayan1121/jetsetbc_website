3. Can you help with a business license or Ejari registration?
Yes, our team provides support for both business license setup and Ejari registration to ensure a smooth and compliant process.

COWORKING PAGE 


1. Can I choose between hot desk and dedicated desk options at Jetset Business Center in Dubai Business Bay?
 Yes, Jetset offers both hot desk and dedicated desk options, giving you the flexibility to choose the workspace that suits your needs.

2. Is it possible to rent a coworking desk on a daily, monthly, or annual basis in Dubai?
 Absolutely! Jetset provides flexible coworking plans, allowing you to rent a desk daily, monthly, or annually in Dubai Business Bay.

3. Do you offer private cabins in addition to coworking desks?
 Yes, Jetset offers private cabins in addition to coworking desks, ideal for teams or individuals seeking more privacy and a dedicated workspace.

4. Can I use Jetset Business Center’s address as my official business address in Dubai?
 Yes, with an Ejari, you can use Jetset Business Center’s address as your official business address in Dubai, making it a valid location for your company registration.


MEETING ROOMS


   1. Can I customize the meeting room setup (boardroom, classroom, U-shape) to fit my event?
Yes, our meeting rooms come with a standard setup, but we can reconfigure the arrangement to match your event requirements.

   2. Is it possible to host hybrid meetings with both in-person and virtual attendees?
Absolutely! Our meeting rooms are designed to support hybrid meetings, with smart TVs and connectivity options for remote participants.

   3. Can I brand the meeting room with my company logo or presentation materials for clients?
Yes, we can help display your company logo or set up presentation materials during your booking.

   4. Can I book last-minute meeting rooms in Dubai if a client visit comes up unexpectedly?
Yes, last-minute bookings are welcome, subject to availability.


BUSINESS SET UP PAGE
      1. Do I need a local partner or sponsor to open a business in Dubai?
 Foreigners can now fully own many businesses in Dubai. Mainland companies may require a local partner or service agent for certain activities, while Free Zone companies allow 100% foreign ownership.


         2. Can foreigners fully own their business in Dubai?
 Yes, Free Zone companies allow 100% foreign ownership. Mainland businesses can also be fully foreign-owned for most activities, with some exceptions.


            3. What types of business licenses are available in Dubai?
 Dubai offers different licenses based on business activity: Commercial (trading), Professional (services), Industrial (manufacturing), and Tourism (travel and hospitality).


               4. Does Jetset provide ongoing support after my company is set up?
 Yes. Jetset offers post-setup services, including PRO support, license renewals, and business advisory to ensure your company runs smoothly in Dubai.
